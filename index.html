Overview

Godrej Lakeside Orchard - Premium Apartments on Sarjapur Road, Bangalore

Experience elegant lakeside living with 3, 3.5, and 4.5 BHK homes featuring private balconies and scenic views. With just 4 homes per floor and minimal shared walls, privacy is a priority. Enjoy 6 acres of open space, over 700 trees, and a 1.9-acre podium garden. The project offers 40+ top-class amenities including a gym, swimming pool, and butterfly park. Located near top schools, hospitals, malls, and tech parks, with excellent connectivity to Carmelaram Railway Station and the upcoming metro line.
